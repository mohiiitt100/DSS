About The Artist

Piyush Sanwaliya Art Wala

Welcome to Piyush Sanwaliya Art Wala, where tradition, devotion, and creativity come together to create timeless masterpieces. Based in Bajrang Nagar, Indore (M.P.), we are dedicated to crafting premium quality artworks that reflect spiritual beauty and Indian heritage.

At our studio, we specialize in:

  • All types of handcrafted idols of Gods and Goddesses
  • Beautifully designed custom mandirs (temples)
  • Premium photo frames with artistic finishing
  • 3D and 4D artwork that brings your vision to life
  • Unique and exclusive art pieces tailored to your taste

    • Every piece we create is not just an object — it's a symbol of devotion, culture, and fine craftsmanship. Our commitment to quality and detail has earned us the trust of countless satisfied customers across the region.
